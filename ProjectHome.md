# What it does? #
Simulates gravity, try to show the gravity efects with planets and moons.


---


# What is cool about #
You can control the zoom and the simulation axis.


---


# Can I control something? #
> Yes.
  * (o) zoom out;
  * (i) zoom in;
  * (w) move up;
  * (s) move down;
  * (d) move right;
  * (a) move left;
